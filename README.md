## 👨‍🔬 About Me

- 🎓 I am a PhD candidate in **Industrial Engineering** at **Dalhousie University**.
- 🔭 My research focuses on complex problem-solving, including **bi-level optimization, evolutionary game theory, and supply chain logistics**.
- 📊 I have a strong focus on **data analysis**, frequently utilizing **SQL** and **Python (Pandas, NumPy)** alongside visualization tools like **Tableau**, **Matplotlib**, and **Seaborn** to query large datasets and interpret complex models.
- 📝 I recently published my work on transportation logistics in *Transportation Research Part E*.
- 💻 I am passionate about bridging the gap between theoretical optimization models and **real-world data analysis**.
- 📍 Based in **Halifax, Nova Scotia**.
